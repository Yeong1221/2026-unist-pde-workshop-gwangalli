Overview

The 2026 UNIST PDE Workshop Gwangalli is organized to share recent research outcomes in mathematical sciences at UNIST and to promote academic exchange on recent developments in partial differential equations and related areas. The workshop provides a forum for research presentations, invited lectures, free-topic discussions, and scholarly interaction among participating researchers.

In particular, the workshop brings together UNIST faculty members, invited researchers, postdoctoral researchers, and graduate students to discuss current research problems and methods, explore possibilities for future collaboration, and exchange views on research directions. Through these activities, the workshop aims to strengthen the research network among participants and to support continuing academic interaction in PDE and mathematical sciences.
